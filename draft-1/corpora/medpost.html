MedPost

Year: 2004

Homepage: ftp://ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pub/lsmith/MedPost/

License: Public domain

The MedPost corpus consists of 5700 sentences from PubMed abstracts annotated for part of speech tags.

Publications:

  • Smith, L and Rindflesch, Thomas and Wilbur, W John et al. (2004). MedPost: a part-of-speech tagger for bioMedical text. Bioinformatics. http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/staff/lsmith/papers/smith04a.pdf
