BioNLP ST 2009

Year: 2008

Homepage: http://www-tsujii.is.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp/GENIA/SharedTask/

License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)

The BioNLP Shared Task 2009 corpus consists of 1210 abstracts annotated for entities and events. The corpus was created based on the annotations of the GENIA event corpus, including an unreleased (blind) set used for evaluation.
