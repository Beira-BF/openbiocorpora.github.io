NCBI disease

Year: 2012

Homepage: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/CBBresearch/Dogan/DISEASE/

License: Public domain

The NCBI disease corpus consists of PubMed abstracts annotated for entities.

Note: the data is public domain as a US government work (Rezarta Islamaj Doğan, personal communication)

Publications:

  • Doğan, Rezarta Islamaj and Leaman, Robert and Lu, Zhiyong (2014). NCBI disease corpus: a resource for disease name recognition and concept normalization. Journal of biomedical informatics. http://europepmc.org/articles/pmc3951655
