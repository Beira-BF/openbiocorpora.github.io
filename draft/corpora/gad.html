GAD

Year: 2015(?)

Homepage: http://ibi.imim.es/research-lines/biomedical-text-mining/corpora/

License: Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbl)

The Genetic Association Database (GAD) corpus consists of sentences annotated for gene and disease entities and their relations.

Note: the name “GAD corpus” is also used for a different corpus in http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-2105/10/S15/S9.

Publications:

  • Bravo, Àlex and Piñero, Janet and Queralt-Rosinach, Núria and Rautschka, Michael and Furlong, Laura I (2015). Extraction of relations between genes and diseases from text and large-scale data analysis: implications for translational research. BMC bioinformatics. http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-2105/16/55
