BioCreative I task 1a (GM)

Year: 2003

Homepage: http://www.biocreative.org/tasks/biocreative-i/first-task-gm/

License: Public domain

The BioCreative I task 1A corpus, also known as the BioCreative I Gene Mention (GM) corpus, consists of 15,000 sentences from PubMed abstracts annotated for mentions of genes and related entities.

Related corpora:

  • BioCreative I task 1a (GM) is extended by BioCreative II task 1a (GM)

Publications:

  • Yeh, Alexander and Morgan, Alexander and Colosimo, Marc and Hirschman, Lynette (2005). BioCreAtIvE task 1A: gene mention finding evaluation. BMC bioinformatics. http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-2105/6/S1/S2
