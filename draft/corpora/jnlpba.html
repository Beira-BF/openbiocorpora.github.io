JNLPBA

Year: 2004

Homepage: http://www-tsujii.is.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp/GENIA/ERtask/report.html

License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)

The JNLPBA (Joint Workshop on Natural Language Processing in Biomedicine and its Applications) corpus consists of 2404 PubMed abstracts annotated for entity mentions.

The JNLPBA corpus was created based on GENIA corpus annotations and used in the JNLPBA / BioNLP 2004 shared task.

Publications:

  • Kim, Jin-Dong and Ohta, Tomoko and Tsuruoka, Yoshimasa and Tateisi, Yuka and Collier, Nigel (2004). Introduction to the bio-entity recognition task at JNLPBA. Proceedings of the international joint workshop on natural language processing in biomedicine and its applications. http://www.anthology.aclweb.org/W/W04/W04-1213.pdf
