BioCreative II task 1a (GM)

Year: 2006

Homepage: http://www.biocreative.org/tasks/biocreative-ii/task-1a-gene-mention-tagging/

License: Public domain

The BioCreative II task 1a corpus, also known as the BioCreative II Gene Mention (GM) tagging corpus, consists of 20,000 sentences from PubMed abstracts annotated for mentions of genes and gene products.

Related corpora:

  • BioCreative II task 1a (GM) extends BioCreative I task 1a (GM)
