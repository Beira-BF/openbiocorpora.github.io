AnEM

Year: 2012

Homepage: http://www.nactem.ac.uk/anatomy/

License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA)

The AnEM (Anatomical Entity Mention) corpus consists of 500 texts (abstracts and full-text extracts) annotated for mentions of anatomical entities.

Related corpora:

  • AnEM is extended by AnatEM

Publications:
