GENIA

Year: 2002

Homepage: http://www.geniaproject.org/

License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)

The GENIA corpus contains several syntactic and semantic annotation layers primarily covering a set of 2,000 abstracts relevant to the query terms human, blood cells, and transcription factors.
