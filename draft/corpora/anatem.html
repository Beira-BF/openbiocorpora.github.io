AnatEM

Year: 2014

Homepage: http://nactem.ac.uk/anatomytagger/

License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA)

The AnatEM (extended Anatomical Entity Mention) corpus consists of 1212 texts (abstracts and full-text extracts) annotated for mentions of anatomical entities.
