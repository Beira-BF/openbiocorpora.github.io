CRAFT

Year: 2012

Homepage: http://bionlp-corpora.sourceforge.net/CRAFT/

License: Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY)

The Colorado Richly Annotated Full Text Corpus (CRAFT) consists of 67 full-text articles from the PMC Open Access subset annotated for syntax and concept mentions.
